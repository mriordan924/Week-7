Pete and Michael Cravenho are 3rd generation painters who have been part of their family business since the day they could pick up a brush, often spending their summer's along side their father and grandfather. They take great pride in the work they do!

Pete Cravenho

In addition to running the daily ins and outs of the business, Pete is a family man living in Lakeville with his wife and 3 kids. Pete is an avid sportsfan, with a particular passion for baseball. Pete spends much of his free time coaching his son's baseball team.

Michael Cravenho

Michael has an eye for the details, so you can be sure that from your living room to your back deck, his work will be perfection! When he's not bringing new life to your home or business, Michael can be found spending time with his Fiance and their two cute cats. Michael has a wide variety of interests, from fearlously working the crowd at karaoke to taking solo trips to Hawaii, he's always up for fun and adventure!
